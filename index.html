About Me

My name is Vincent Nguyen. I graduated from UW Tacoma in 2024 with a bachelor's degree in Information Technology and have a passion for web development. With a solid academic background and hands-on experience with both front-end and back-end development, I hope to find a career in creating efficient and user-friendly web applications! I've had the opportunity to work on a couple of projects related to web development, including designing an Asset Management System for Port Madison Enterprise and a Stall Management System for the YMCA. These experiences have contributed to enhancing my skills in HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, and other programming languages, as well as my ability to design responsive user interfaces. My previous internship as a Teaching Assistant for the GenCyber program allowed me to share my knowledge of cybersecurity principles with a wide audience, which solidified my passion for technology and education. On this website, you will find more details about my work experience, projects, technical skills, and more.
